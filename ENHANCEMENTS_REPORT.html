Four Volume Directions

üü¢ ACCUMULATION

Signal: Rising price + High volume

Meaning: Smart money buying

Action: Boost confidence, hold position longer

Example: Stock up 2%, volume +45%

üî¥ DISTRIBUTION

Signal: High volume with uncertain price

Meaning: Smart money selling

Action: Reduce confidence, exit early

Example: Stock up 1%, volume -30%

‚ö†Ô∏è DIVERGENCE

Signal: Falling price + Low volume

Meaning: Weak selling, reversal likely

Action: Watch for bounce, avoid shorts

Example: Stock down 2%, volume -40%

‚ö™ NEUTRAL

Signal: Volume within normal range

Meaning: Unclear direction

Action: Wait for clearer signal

How It Works

When you see: 1. Price up + Volume UP ‚Üí ACCUMULATION (97% confidence) 2. Price up + Volume DOWN ‚Üí DISTRIBUTION (85% confidence warning!) 3. Price down + Volume DOWN ‚Üí DIVERGENCE (60% reversal alert!) 4. Unclear signals ‚Üí NEUTRAL (wait for confirmation)
